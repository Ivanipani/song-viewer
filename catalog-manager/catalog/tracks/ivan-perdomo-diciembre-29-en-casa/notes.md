# Diciembre 29 en casa

## Overview
A reflective piece recorded at home on December 29th. This track captures a moment of quiet contemplation during the holiday season.

## Performance Notes
- This piece has a relaxed, intimate feel - maintain a gentle touch throughout
- Let notes ring out and breathe between phrases
- The recording intentionally preserves the raw, unpolished quality of the performance

## Context
Recorded during the quiet days between Christmas and New Year's, when time seems to slow down and there's space for introspection.

## Recording Philosophy
This track embraces the imperfections and spontaneity of the moment. It's more about capturing a feeling than achieving technical perfection.
