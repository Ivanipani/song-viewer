# Dia de Sol

## Overview
A bright, uplifting piece in G major that evokes the warmth and energy of a sunny day.

## Performance Notes
- Keep the rhythm light and bouncy to maintain the cheerful character
- The I-V-ii progression creates a circular, uplifting motion
- Emphasize the top voice in the chord voicings to bring out the melody
- Use dynamic variation to create interest throughout the piece

## Technical Details
The piece uses a simple but effective three-chord progression:
- **I (G)** - The home chord, bright and stable
- **V (D)** - Creates movement and tension
- **ii (Am)** - Adds color before resolving back to I

## Mood and Character
This piece captures the feeling of stepping outside on a beautiful day - optimistic, energetic, and full of possibility.
