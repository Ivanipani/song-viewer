# Busqueda

## Overview
An introspective piece exploring themes of searching and self-discovery. The piece centers around the key of A minor, creating a contemplative mood.

## Performance Notes
- This was an improvised jam session that captured a particularly inspired moment
- The fingerpicking pattern follows a steady 4/4 time signature
- Focus on dynamics - start soft and build gradually through the piece
- Pay attention to the palm muting technique in the verses for contrast

## Inspiration
This piece came together during a quiet morning session. The mood was contemplative, thinking about life paths and choices.

## Key Sections
1. **Intro** (0:00-0:30) - Establishes the Am tonality with fingerpicking
2. **Main Theme** (0:30-1:30) - Introduction of the primary melodic motif
3. **Development** (1:30-2:30) - Exploration with added harmonic movement
4. **Resolution** (2:30-end) - Return to the opening theme with variations
